# Number Series Problems

This document contains number series problems used to improve logical reasoning and pattern recognition skills.

---

## Problem 1: Simple Arithmetic Series

### Question

Find the next number in the series:

2, 4, 6, 8, ?

### Answer

10

### Explanation

The pattern increases by **+2 each time**.

---

## Problem 2: Square Numbers

### Question

Find the missing number:

1, 4, 9, 16, ?

### Answer

25

### Explanation

These are **square numbers**.

1² = 1
2² = 4
3² = 9
4² = 16
5² = 25

---

## Problem 3: Increasing Difference

### Question

Find the next number:

3, 7, 13, 21, ?

### Answer

31

### Explanation

The difference increases by 2.

7 - 3 = 4
13 - 7 = 6
21 - 13 = 8
Next difference = 10

21 + 10 = **31**

## Problem 4: Fibonacci Pattern

### Question

Find the next number:

1, 1, 2, 3, 5, 8, ?

### Answer

13

### Explanation

Each number is the **sum of the previous two numbers**.

## Problem 5: Alternate Pattern

### Question

Find the missing number:

2, 5, 4, 7, 6, ?

### Answer

9

### Explanation

Two patterns alternate.

+3 → 2 → 5 → 7 → 9
-1 → 5 → 4 → 6

## Problem 10: Decreasing Pattern

### Question

Find the next number:

100, 95, 85, 70, ?

### Answer

50

### Explanation

Differences:

-5
-10
-15
Next difference = -20

70 - 20 = **50**
