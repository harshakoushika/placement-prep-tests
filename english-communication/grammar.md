# English Grammar Notes

This file contains important English grammar concepts with examples.

---

## 1. Parts of Speech

### Noun

A noun is the name of a person, place, thing, or idea.

Examples:

* Student
* City
* Computer

Example sentence:
She is a **student**.

---

### Verb

A verb describes an action or state.

Examples:

* run
* eat
* write

Sentence:
He **writes** code.

---

## 2. Tenses

### Present Tense

Describes actions happening now.

Example:
She **studies** every day.

### Past Tense

Describes actions that already happened.

Example:
She **studied** yesterday.

### Future Tense

Describes actions that will happen.

Example:
She **will study** tomorrow.

---

## Practice Questions

1. Identify the noun in the sentence:
   "The programmer fixed the bug."

2. Change the sentence to past tense:
   "She writes a report."

Answers:

1. programmer, bug
2. She wrote a report.
