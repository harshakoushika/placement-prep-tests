# Reading Comprehension

This file contains passages and questions to improve reading and understanding skills.

---

## Passage 1

Technology has transformed the way people communicate.
Today, messages can be sent instantly through the internet, allowing people across the world to stay connected.

### Questions

1. What has transformed communication?
2. How can people send messages today?

### Answers

1. Technology
2. Through the internet

## Passage 2

Artificial Intelligence is changing many industries.
It helps automate tasks, analyze large amounts of data, and assist humans in decision making.

### Questions

1. What is changing many industries?
2. What tasks can AI perform?

### Answers

1. Artificial Intelligence
2. Automation, data analysis, and decision assistance
